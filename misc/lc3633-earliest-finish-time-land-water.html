GT LEARN #3633 🎢 Earliest Finish Time for Land & Water Rides I MEDIUM Greedy Array Brute Force
lStart= lDur= wStart= wDur= exp: 9
📝 Problem Statement

Tourist ko EXACTLY 1 land ride aur 1 water ride dono lene hain (kisi bhi order mein). Har ride i ka start[i] hai (sabse pehle kab open hota hai) aur duration[i] hai (kitna lagta hai). Agar tourist time t pe ride pe pohncha, toh ride max(t, start[i]) + duration[i] pe khatam hogi. Goal: minimum possible finish time jab dono rides ho jaayein.

🧠 Solving Approach

Brute force: dono orders try karo — Land→Water aur Water→Land. Har pair (i, j) ke liye finish time compute karo. Pehli ride normal time pe khatam hoti hai; doosri ride ka start max(prevFinish, start[j]) se hota hai. Sab combinations ka min hi answer hai.

⏱️ End time formula
end = max(arrival, start) + duration — agar early aaye toh wait, late aaye toh straight start.
🔄 Two orders
Phase 1: Land first, then Water. Phase 2: Water first, then Land. Min of both = answer.
🏆 Min tracking
Initialize ans = ∞, har candidate ke saath ans = min(ans, candidate).
⏱ Time: O(n·m) 💾 Space: O(1)
