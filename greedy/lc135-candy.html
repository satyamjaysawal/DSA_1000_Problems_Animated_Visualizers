ratings= exp: 5

📝 Problem Statement

n bachhe ek line mein khade hain. Har bachhe ka ek rating value hai (ratings[i]). Tumhe candies distribute karni hain in 2 rules ke saath:

1️⃣ Har bachhe ko at least 1 candy milni chahiye.
2️⃣ Jis bachhe ki rating apne neighbor (left ya right) se zyada hai, usse strictly more candies milni chahiye.

Return minimum total candies needed.

🧠 Solving Approach — Two-Pass Greedy

Single pass se dono neighbors satisfy nahi ho sakte. Toh hum do passes karte hain:

Pass 1 (Left → Right): Sirf left neighbor compare karo. Agar ratings[i] > ratings[i-1], toh candies[i] = candies[i-1] + 1.
Pass 2 (Right → Left): Right neighbor compare karo. Agar ratings[i] > ratings[i+1], toh candies[i] = max(candies[i], candies[i+1] + 1) — max isliye taaki Pass 1 ka constraint na toote!
Base: Sab candies = [1,1,...,1] se start. Sum at end = answer.
⏱️ Time: O(n) 💾 Space: O(n)
