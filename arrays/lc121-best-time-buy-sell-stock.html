expected: 5

📝 Problem Statement

Diya gaya array prices jahan prices[i] us din ka stock price hai. Sirf ek transaction allowed hai — pehle buy, baad mein sell (sell day > buy day). Maximum profit nikalo. Agar koi profit possible nahi, return 0.

🧠 Solving Approach

Single-pass greedy approach: array ko left-to-right scan karte hue do cheezein track karo — ab tak ka minimum price (best buy day) aur max profit. Har day pe sochna: "agar yahan se becho, kitna profit?" = price - minPrice. Iska maximum hi answer hai.

🎯 Core Concepts

Min So FarHar step pe lowest seen price track — yahi best "buy day" candidate.
Profit Calcprice[i] - minSoFar = current best sell ka profit.
Max TrackingSabhi candidate profits ka max hi final answer banta hai.

⏱ Complexity

Time: O(n) Space: O(1)
