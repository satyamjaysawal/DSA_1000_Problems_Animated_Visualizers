GT LEARN #1944 👥 Number of Visible People in Queue HARD Monotonic Stack
exp: [3,1,2,1,1,0]

📝 Problem Statement

There are n people standing in a queue. You are given an array heights. A person can see another person to their right in the queue if everybody in between is shorter than both of them. Return an array where answer[i] is the number of people the i-th person can see.

🧠 Solving Approach

We traverse from right to left. We maintain a Monotonic Stack that keeps heights in decreasing order. If the current person is taller than the stack's top, those shorter people are "popped" (they are visible but blocked for anyone further left). If the stack still has someone after popping, that person is also visible (the first one who blocks the view).

Right-to-Left Process from end so we know who is ahead.
Pop Shorter Remove people shorter than current (they are visible but blocked).
Count Blocker If stack not empty after pops, add 1 (the blocker is visible).
Time: O(N) Space: O(N)
